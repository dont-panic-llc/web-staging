Who We Are

Don’t Panic Consulting, LLC, (Don’t Panic) is a Maine, USA based technology, business and digital marketing, managed services provider with our own software stack.

Where We Came From

Our roots are in consulting services, development of advanced technologies, and business process solutions.

What We're Doing

Don’t Panic is shaping better and more integrated software and business solutions designed to improve customer experience and efficiency.

Our Belief System

Don’t Panic believes that complex, cutting edge technology usually reserved for large organizations, should be available to all organizations at a reasonable price. Our expert consulting services and packages are designed to complement and enhance your own business operations and revenue growth.

Why Us What We're Trying To Solve

Complicated business systems - Business tools are usually complicated, and difficult to maintain, requiring teams of administrators and developers to tailor to each business. For small businesses, this is a huge drain on tight resources.

Expensive - Business tools are expensive to deploy, costing thousands to millions of dollars per year to maintain and store data in. This is prohibitive to small businesses, and painful for medium businesses to handle.

Fragmented business tools - - Many tools are needed to conduct business; Enterprise Resource Planning, Customer Relationship Management, Case Management, Knowledge Base, Accounting, Inventory, all with their own price tags. No company currently builds in all of the business tools for CRM, ERP, and data-driven decision making.

Customizable Intuitive Design

Whether it be our individual consulting or marketing services, or our intuitive software stack, we have simple solutions and tools designed to eliminate the need for the current complicated market solutions.

Easy to Use, Easy to Manage

Ease of use is critical to business user adoption. Ease of management is critical for business administrators peace of mind. No one AM phone calls, and no complexity in user interface. Consistent and efficient business operations coupled with ease of user adoption ensure that costs are contained and revenues increased.

Unified Business Tools

Instead of 3+ tools to run a business, just use the Eden Business Management Suite - One tool for all of your business needs to help you sell, manage, and grow. Eden is fully customizable to your business and ensures all your sales-staff collect the information you need on every lead. Ensures your support staff properly document cases.
